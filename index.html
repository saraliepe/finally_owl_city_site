Owl City

Life is Cinematic

Adam Young, who releases music under the name Owl City, came to his international fame in 2009 with the release of his single Fireflies. He might seem to many as a one-hit-wonder, but Young has been continuing to release successful music over the last decade. He has released 6 studio albums, 7 EP's, and 21 singles. He also has 11 instrumental albums that he release in 2016 under the name Adam Young that narrate important events in his life and in history: the sinking of the Titanic, Apollo 11, and more. Young's most recent release was his album titled "Cinematic" in the summer of 2018 which lead to an international tour.
